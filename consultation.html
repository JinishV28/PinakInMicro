Consultation

At “PinakInMicro”, we offer expert microbiology consulting services to enhance and protect businesses through -

Audits & Inspections⯈
Preparing laboratory and facilities for Regulatory and Quality Compliance audits/inspections
Speed to Markets⯈
Microbiological controls in manufacturing and releasing quality products rapidly (Rapid microbiological methods, PAT, Parametric releases) to the markets.
Design robustness assessments⯈
Scientific risk model-based assessment of manufacturing facili ties, formulations, Raw materials and packaging materials
Failure Investigations & resolutions⯈
Marketed Product failure investigations, CAPA and risk assessment for Objectionable organisms
Training & coaching⯈
Prepare teams and organization for Lab of the future/future generation of microbiologists
Claims substantiation⯈
Creating and supporting new microbiological claims on the products (including claims for prebiotics, probiotics, preservative free)
Sterilization process controls⯈
Microbiological Qualification of sterilization processes for Steam, Gamma,Ethylene oxide etc.
